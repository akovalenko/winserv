2005-08-19 Winserv 1.10 released

    * BUG FIXED:
    Winserv used to crash after receiving some non-STOP signal
    with -ipcmethod blind (e.g. when the service dependencies were changed)
    Thank Ashok Singhal for pointing it out and providing a patch.

    * IMPROVEMENT:
    Winserv is trying more gentle shutdown method before TerminateProcess
    with -ipcmethod blind. (TECHNICAL: it tries to post WM_QUIT message 
    to the initial thread's queue of the underlying process, and if it's possible,
    waits for 10 seconds allowing the process to exit gracefully).

    * IMPROVEMENT:
    Console applications now survive user's logout without additional effort.
    (CREATE_NEW_PROCESS_GROUP flag was used to achieve this). The only exception
    is the applications that set their own console Ctrl handler.

    * BUILDING:
    You may use GNU toolchain (MINGW32) to compile winserv.
    It may be cross-compiled as well.
